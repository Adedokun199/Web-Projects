About Us

GTX is a decentralized crypto exchange that is designed to provide users with a secure, fast and reliable trading experience.

With GTX, users can trade digital assets instantly, without the need for a middleman or third party. This eliminates the need for trust in a centralized authority and ensures that transactions are conducted securely and at a low cost. Additionally, GTX offers advanced features and tools such as Order Book, Price Alerts, and Trading Bots which aid traders in making informed decisions about their trades. This makes GTX a powerful platform for anyone looking to trade digital assets with confidence and ease.
